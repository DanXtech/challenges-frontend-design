COMPANY POLICIES

QUALITY POLICY We are committed to consistently provide Product and Services that will meet and possibly exceed the Quality standards set by our clients. At ATLANTURE, We will actively focus on improving quality through training and programs that enable our employee to do their job first time and every time We understand and accept that each project is unique in requirements, and are committed to ensuring our customer expectations and targets are met at all time .

HSE POLICY Standard safety rules and practice are imbibed in ATLANTURE Safety of workers and safe working environment is our priority. Safety attitudes are developed in workers to focus their attention on ways to avert possible accidents. At ATLANTURE , the policy of developmental safety work habit and the danger of handling equipment tools and machinery are given its safety regulation namely:

TRAINING: intensive staff training on safety and use of equipment and machineries to develop a positive mental attitude toward safety regulation is vigorously perused.

EMPLOYMENT POLICY REGULATION AND SAFETY only people with minimum education qualification and Training that enable them to read and write are employed to enhance our safety regulation.

SUPERVISION strict supervision to be sure that all safety advice guards and other items of protective equipment are in working order and being used at all times and that allowed to use on equipment.

FIRST AID A first aid kit is usually provided on all sites and offices . The kit is periodically checked and replenished and basic training in first aid is given to the safety supervisor and first aiders to give him proper background for assisting and acting in case of injury
